Register for a Programme

Apply online in minutes. Start your digital skills journey with YouthTech Skills Hub today.

How to Apply

  1. Complete the application form below with your personal details and chosen programme.
  2. Submit your application — you will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.
  3. Attend an intake assessment — a short online or in-person session to confirm your eligibility and placement.
  4. Receive your acceptance letter and enrolment instructions ahead of your start date.

Eligibility Requirements

  • South African citizen or permanent resident
  • Aged 18 to 35 years
  • Minimum Grade 10 (or equivalent) qualification
  • Basic English literacy
  • Access to a computer or mobile device (loaner devices are available — indicate this on the form)

If you are unsure whether you qualify, please contact us at info@youthtechskillshub.org.za before applying.
