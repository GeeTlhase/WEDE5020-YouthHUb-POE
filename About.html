About YouthTech Skills Hub

Dedicated to bridging the digital divide and empowering South African youth through skills and education.

Our Story

YouthTech Skills Hub was founded with a single purpose: to address the growing digital divide in South Africa. Recognising that systemic youth unemployment is partly driven by a lack of access to relevant digital skills training, our founders set out to create an organisation that could make a meaningful and lasting difference.

What began as a small community initiative has grown into a fully-fledged non-profit organisation offering a range of certified digital skills programmes. We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to participate in the digital economy, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Today, we are proud to have helped hundreds of young South Africans gain employment, launch businesses, and build futures they once thought were out of reach.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality digital education and skills training that empowers South African youth to overcome unemployment and achieve economic independence.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every young person has the digital competencies needed to participate fully and equally in the modern economy.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: We remove barriers to quality education.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and learning methods.
  • Community: We build strong networks among learners and employers.
  • Integrity: We operate transparently and hold ourselves accountable.
  • Impact: We measure our success by the change we create in people's lives.
