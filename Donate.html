Support YouthTech Skills Hub

Your donation directly funds digital skills training for young South Africans, helping them build careers and break the cycle of unemployment.

Why Your Support Matters

South Africa faces one of the highest youth unemployment rates in the world. By donating to YouthTech Skills Hub, you are directly investing in the future of our country's young people. Every rand raised goes towards course materials, qualified instructors, device access programmes, and learner support services.

  • R 500 covers learning materials for one student for an entire course.
  • R 1,500 provides a student with a loaner laptop for the duration of their programme.
  • R 5,000 fully sponsors one student's enrolment — from application through to graduation.
  • R 22,000 funds a full cohort of 20 students for one short course.

Other Ways to Give

Corporate Sponsorship

Partner with us as a corporate sponsor. We offer naming rights for cohorts, branded materials, and CSI reporting support. Contact us at partnerships@youthtechskillshub.org.za to discuss sponsorship packages.

Donate Equipment

We gratefully accept donations of laptops, tablets, and other functional devices. Donated equipment goes directly to learners who do not have access to their own devices. Contact us to arrange a device drop-off or collection.

Volunteer Your Skills

Industry professionals can contribute by volunteering as guest lecturers, mentors, or curriculum advisors. If you have expertise in a digital field and a passion for youth development, we would love to hear from you.

How We Use Your Donation

We are committed to full financial transparency. Below is how every rand donated is allocated:

Category Allocation
Programme delivery (instruction, materials, facilities) 65%
Learner support (devices, data, transport grants) 20%
Operations and administration 10%
Fundraising and communications 5%

YouthTech Skills Hub is a registered non-profit organisation (NPO). Donations may qualify for Section 18A tax deductibility. Please contact us for a formal receipt.
